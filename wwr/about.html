Happy Rapids Rafting

The Happy Rapids Rafting adjusts to the age of the participants, the philosophy is the same as if it were a children's summer camp: a program of activities and workshops so you don't have to sit still for a minute and monitors who organize and supervise the groups to everything works like a charm

History

Founded in 1995, RiverPulse Rafting began as a small family venture nestled along the banks of the roaring Colorado River. Inspired by a love for nature and a passion for adventure, the company was born to provide thrilling whitewater experiences while fostering a deep respect for the environment.
